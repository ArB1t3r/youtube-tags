Welcome to YouTube Tags

Organize your YouTube subscriptions with custom tags.
Let's get you set up in under a minute.

Step 1 — Create your tags

Tags are categories you'll use to organize channels. We've prepared some defaults — feel free to edit, remove, or add your own.

Step 2 — Detect your subscriptions

Open your YouTube channels page — the extension will automatically scroll and detect all your subscriptions.

0 channels detected

The page will auto-scroll to load all channels. Wait until the count stops increasing, then come back here.

Step 3 — How to tag channels

There are two ways to assign tags to your channels:

📋

Options Page

Open the full management page to see all detected channels. Click any channel to assign tags. Great for bulk organizing.

▶️

While Watching

When watching a video, click the Tags button below the video title to quickly tag that channel.

💡 Once channels are tagged, use the extension popup to filter your feed — only see videos from the tags you pick.

Step 4 — AI tag suggestions optional

Got hundreds of subscriptions? Let AI suggest which tags fit each channel. You'll need an API key from OpenAI or Anthropic.

🎉

You're all set!

Your tags are ready. Start browsing YouTube and organize your channels as you go.
